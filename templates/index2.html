What is gigantazon.com?

The mission of gigantazon.com is to make ideas actionable. It is a community platform that maps online conversations and encourages engagement from a like-minded community, what we like to call the Do Anything Crowd.

The Do Anything Crowd pulls from their 3 Es (Experience, Expertise, and Education) to consider ideas, and build on them to make them happen in the real world.

Our SIA Platform (which stands for Sparks/Ideas/Actions), maps the segments of Human-Centered Design to make them digestible by the users. So, Sparks are similar to the Inspiration phase in Design Thinking – what is the support material for the idea, be it the inspiration of the original idea, or additional links to outside material found along the way. Ideas are essentially what you would find in the Ideation category – fields available for people to comment and move the conversation along. That could be a statement of opinion, personal experience with similar ideas, or asking clarifying questions. Actions are most closely aligned with the Implementation process – they could be deadlines for the team around an idea to have certain things accomplished, like research for the project, a team meeting, or even a virtual get together to discuss further.

The contributions, regardless of nature, are mapped out in relationship to the particular idea. As that happens, you’re able to watch the conversation grow as these branches take on a life of their own.

How is this different from any other website?

Even the most innovative of forums continue to use the same old technology that came out at the World Wide Web’s inception – chronologically listing contributions. Even with improved nesting functionality, the results are difficult to pore over once they reach a particular size.

By mapping the contributions, they are now tied to other concepts by topic, not timing. The asynchronous nature of the tool means that anyone can build off of any contribution, not just the most recent ones.

Why should I join?

How many great ideas have you had that you simply haven’t had the time, knowledge, or ability to bring to life? Leverage the resources of the Do Anything Crowd and make them happen!

As the tool to make ideas actionable for social good, gigantazon.com is the future of online communication. Our members determine how to maximize their contributions, whether by Sparking inspired Ideas or make them happen through their Actions. We are where members come together to make the world a better place.
