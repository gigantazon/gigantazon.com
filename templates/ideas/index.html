A few reasons you'll love Gigantazon

Online Conversation

An online conversation like you've never seen it before. Now you can really express yourself - link to an article to show context, comment to share your opinion, or participate in a live meeting. It's all there.

Intuitive Communication

FINALLY! A tool that closely mimics in-person communication. Instead of that linear, chronological, Roman-numeral type outline that we're used to seeing, you can watch the conversation grow visually.

True Collaboration

No joke - this site was built with collaboration in mind. Follow the ideas you like, and keep coming back and bring them to life! These ideas need your help to be realized.

Crowd Innovation

Sure, you could invite your friends to build on your idea - and you should! - but you can also get instant feedback from the Gigantazon community.
