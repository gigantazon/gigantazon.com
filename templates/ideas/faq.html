What kinds of ideas should I use or NOT use?
  1. We obviously don't encourage anything hateful or derogatory. Please don't use our website as your personal toilet.
  2. Great ideas are problems that need solving. New ways of doing things, or different ways of thinking.
  3. Within reason, no ideas are off limits

Who can see my ideas?

  2. Public ideas are seen by any user of the website
